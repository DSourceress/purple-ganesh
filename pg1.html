Games with Purple Ganesh

  • Hide The Monkey
  • Green Pantry Raid
  • Accessorize Ganesh

Rules for Hide The Monkey

  1. Obtain an opponent
  2. Bring your monkey to your opponent's house
  3. Put the monkey out of sight of the opponent
  4. Laugh raucously as your monkey destroys your opponents home

Rules for Green Pantry Raid

  1. Obtain 11 cohorts
  2. Obtain a dozen Greenman suits
  3. Don the Greenman suits
  4. Get collecting

Rules for Accessorize Ganesh

  1. Break out Ganesh's trunk
  2. Apply blindfold to each player
  3. In the style of "Pin the Tail on the Donkey" adorn Ganesh as desired
